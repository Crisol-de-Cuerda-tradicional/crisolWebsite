For any questions or information about Crisol, you can write to us at [info@crisoldecuerda.com](<(mailto:info@crisoldecuerda.com)>) and we'll be happy to help.

Please bear in mind that Crisol is run by volunteers who dedicate their free time to the project. If we take a little while to reply, we appreciate your patience.

And if you'd like to keep up with our latest news, subscribe to our [newsletter](https://crisoldecuerda.us17.list-manage.com/subscribe?u=d41fb451e84e5b0e26aba00a2&id=1d1b5b5fc1)!
