Gonzalo Llao is an Asturian flute and bouzouki player. From his earliest steps, alongside training in early music, he began to explore Asturian music and then the traditional music of the Celtic nations. That dedication has shaped him into an eclectic, wide-ranging musician on flute, whistle, banjo and bouzouki. His professional folk career began with **Vrienden**, an Asturian folk-fusion group with whom he recorded their second album, *UVE*. He is now a member of **Deira**, where he developed a sound on the album *Alba* that blends electronic and contemporary colours with tradition and modernity.

He specialises in early music and holds a degree in recorder from the Conservatorio Superior de Zaragoza. Alongside performing, he teaches widely on flutes and string instruments.
