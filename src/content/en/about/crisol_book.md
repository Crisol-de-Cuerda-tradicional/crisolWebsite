The Crisol Book was compiled in 2018 to commemorate the 10th anniversary of Crisol de Cuerda. It brings together all the tunes that have been taught at Crisol and could be gathered through audio recordings, videos, sheet music, and handwritten manuscripts. In the spirit of Crisol, we make this collection of scores available to you in the hope that you enjoy learning, playing, and sharing them as much as we have over the years.

The scores found here are transcribed as they were recorded and taught at Crisol de Cuerda. Although there are differences from the original versions of the tunes (or those found in other collections), we have chosen to preserve the variations that, for us, serve as our reference point and reflect the way they are played in our sessions. The information contained in the Crisol Book has been gathered from a variety of sources and may not be 100% accurate. If you spot any errors and can provide information to help us correct them, please don't hesitate to get in touch at [info@crisoldecuerda.com](mailto:info@crisoldecuerda.com?subject=Errata%20en%20Crisol%20Book).

We would not want to close this note without thanking all the teachers who have generously shared their music and knowledge with us over all these years. We hope this book serves as a tribute to the experiences we have lived together and as an inspiration for the many more still to come.
